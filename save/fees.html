Professional Workshops: Fees

Yellow Belt$595/person
Green Belt$2,500/person
Black Belt$7,700/person

Notes

  1. All participant materials (except the statistical analysis software package) are included in the above fee schedule.
  2. Payment is required at least three weeks in advance of the workshop. Credit cards are not accepted.
  3. Workshops for independent individuals are held at Dartmouth College during regular scheduled offerings and are subject to minimum class size requirements.
  4. Travel expenses for custom workshops are additional. Please contact us for details.
  5. Black Belt candidates opting to complete case studies in place of a true project at their place of work will be charged an additional $1,950 per person.
  6. Group pricing may be available upon request.
  7. We reserve the right to cancel our sessions if minimum attendance (five participants) is not met. Workshop fees will be refunded in full.

Student Workshops: Schedule & Fees

The Green Belt workshop is offered during fall and spring terms. The three mandatory lectures and an optional statistics review session are held in the evening, 5 to 7 p.m., at Thayer School of Engineering.

Email Professor Lasky (Ronald.Lasky@Dartmouth.edu) for workshop fees and dates.

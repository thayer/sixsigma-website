What is Six Sigma?

"Six Sigma" is a term applied to advanced process management methods used to improve company performance. Because the methods rest on a base of statistical analysis and problem solving techniques, they require an in-depth education. Many top performing companies, including General Electric, Motorola, 3M Company, Raytheon, AIG, and J.P Morgan Chase have embraced Six Sigma methodologies and have dramatically reduced costs and increased revenues.

Goals of Six Sigma
Reduce defects to 6σ level

Increase yields

Improve customer satisfaction

Increase revenues
Deliver shareholder value and high
return on investment

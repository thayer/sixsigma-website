Professional Workshops: Schedule

Scheduled Workshops

Workshops highlight case studies from both manufacturing and service organizations, presenting robust and applicable content to satisfy virtually every individual.

Live Online Workshops:

These workshops are run regularly at a minimum of 3 times per year.  All participants are welcome as long as they are qualified to meet the entry requirements of having taken a college level statistics workshop before entering the black belt workshop.  There are no entry requirements for yellow belt or green belt other than high school education.  Check curricula for specific requirements.

See the dates when you go to Register online for these workshops.

Standard / Customized:

Workshops are available for groups of 10 or more and are typically held at the client’s site. 

Each workshop can be customized to meet the customer requirements:

  • For Manufacturing Companies
    Workshop modules will focus on the needs of manufacturers and classroom examples will be specific to the industry. The curriculum will include case studies that take into account the unique requirements of your manufacturing challenges.
  • For Service Companies
    Lean Six Sigma can benefit service organizations—where value centers on a person's actions. We understand the service industry and can show you how to improve your practices to achieve substantial cost savings. Workshop topics will mirror your organization's needs.

Contact us to schedule a custom workshop.

Student:

On-campus workshops are available to Dartmouth students only, and are scheduled from time to time as enough interest presents itself.  Contact us for details.  Students may also register for LIVE ONLINE workshops.

Notes

  1. All participant materials (except MiniTab and any specified books) are included in the above fee schedule.
  2. Payment is required at least two weeks in advance of the workshop.
  3. Workshops for independent individuals (except for the online workshops) are held at Dartmouth College during regular scheduled offerings and are subject to minimum class size requirements.
  4. Travel expenses for custom workshops are additional. Please contact us for details.
  5. Black Belt candidates for online workshops must do case studies instead of an actual project.
  6. Group pricing for custom workshops is provided by quotation.
  7. We reserve the right to cancel sessions if minimum attendance is not met. Workshop fees will be refunded in full.
  8. The signup deadline for online workshops is two weeks prior to the starting date.
  9. Online sessions will be recorded if a participant is absent.
